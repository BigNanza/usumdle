How to Play

The goal of this game is to guess a Pokémon from Ultra Sun & Ultra Moon. Every day a new Pokémon is chosen, and by continuing to guess the Pokémon of the day, you can build up a win-streak.

Begin by typing the name of any Pokémon in the search field. Based on your search, up to 10 results will pop up. Click on any of these Pokémon, or navigate to them with your arrow keys and press Enter to lock your guess in.

The properties of your chosen Pokémon will be compared to today's Pokémon and will be marked:

  • Green if matching
  • Yellow for partial matches
  • Red if incorrect

Behind incorrect number fields you will also find an arrow indicating whether you should guess higher or lower.
